## Abstract
Knowledge Graph (KG) embedding has emerged as an active area of research resulting in the development of several KG embedding methods. Relational facts in KG often show temporal dynamics, e.g., the fact (Cristiano Ronaldo, playsFor, Manchester_United) is valid only from 2003 to 2009. Most of the existing KG embedding methods ignore this temporal dimension while learning embeddings of the KG elements. In this paper, we propose HyTE, a temporally aware KG embedding method which explicitly incorporates time in the entity-relation space by associating each timestamp with a corresponding hyperplane. HyTE not only performs KG inference using temporal guidance, but also predicts temporal scopes for relational facts with missing time annotations. Through extensive experimentation on temporal datasets extracted from real-world KGs, we demonstrate the effectiveness of our model over both traditional as well as temporal KG embedding methods.
知識圖譜 (KG) 嵌入已成為一個活躍的研究領域，並催生了數種 KG 嵌入方法的發展。知識圖譜中的關係事實通常表現出時間動態性，例如，事實（克里斯蒂亞諾·羅納爾多，效力於，曼聯）僅在 2003 年至 2009 年期間有效。大多數現有的 KG 嵌入方法在學習 KG 元素的嵌入時都忽略了這個時間維度。在本文中，我們提出了 HyTE，一種時間感知的 KG 嵌入方法，它通過將每個時間戳與對應的超平面相關聯，從而將時間明確地納入實體-關係空間中。HyTE 不僅使用時間指導進行知識圖譜推理，還能預測缺少時間標註的關係事實的時間範圍。通過對從真實世界知識圖譜中提取的時間數據集進行廣泛的實驗，我們證明了我們的模型在傳統以及時間性知識圖譜嵌入方法上都具有有效性。

## 1 Introduction
Knowledge Graphs (KGs) are large multi-relational graphs where nodes correspond to entities, and typed edges represent relationships among them. KGs encode factual beliefs in the form of triple (entity, relation, entity), e.g., (Brussels, isCapitalOf, Belgium). Examples of a few KGs include NELL (Mitchell et al., 2018), YAGO (Suchanek et al., 2007), and Freebase (Bollacker et al., 2008). KGs have been found to be useful for a variety of tasks, viz., Information Retrieval (Kotov and Zhai, 2012; Xiong and Callan, 2015), Question Answering (Dong et al., 2015; Bordes et al., 2015; Yao and Durme, 2014), among others.
知識圖譜 (KGs) 是大型的多重關係圖，其中節點對應實體，帶有類型的邊表示它們之間的關係。KGs 以三元組（實體、關係、實體）的形式編碼事實信念，例如（布魯塞爾，是...的首都，比利時）。一些知識圖譜的例子包括 NELL (Mitchell et al., 2018)、YAGO (Suchanek et al., 2007) 和 Freebase (Bollacker et al., 2008)。知識圖譜已被發現在多種任務中非常有用，例如資訊檢索 (Kotov and Zhai, 2012; Xiong and Callan, 2015)、問答 (Dong et al., 2015; Bordes et al., 2015; Yao and Durme, 2014) 等。
KG embedding has emerged as a very active area of research over the last few years, resulting in the development of several techniques (Bordes et al., 2013; Nickel et al., 2016b; Yang et al., 2014; Lin et al., 2015; Trouillon et al., 2016; Dettmers et al., 2018; Guo et al., 2018). These methods learn high-dimensional vectorial representations for nodes and relations in the KG, while preserving various graph and knowledge constraints.
在過去幾年中，KG 嵌入已成為一個非常活躍的研究領域，催生了數種技術的發展（Bordes et al., 2013; Nickel et al., 2016b; Yang et al., 2014; Lin et al., 2015; Trouillon et al., 2016; Dettmers et al., 2018; Guo et al., 2018）。這些方法學習知識圖譜中節點和關係的高維向量表示，同時保留各種圖和知識約束。
We note that KG beliefs are not universally true, as they tend to be valid only in a specific time period. For example, (Bill Clinton, presidentOf, USA) was true only from 1993 to 2001. KG beliefs with such temporal validity marked are called as temporally scoped. These temporal scopes are increasingly available on several large KGs, e.g., YAGO (Suchanek et al., 2007), Wikidata (Erxleben et al., 2014). The mainstream KG embedding methods ignore the availability or importance of such temporal scopes while learning embeddings of nodes and relations in the KGs. These methods treat the KG as a static graph with the assumption that the beliefs contained in them are universally true. This is clearly inadequate and it is quite conceivable that incorporating temporal scopes during representation learning is likely to yield better KG embeddings. In spite of its importance, temporally aware KG embeddings is a relatively unexplored area. Recently, a KG embedding method which utilizes temporal scopes was proposed in (Jiang et al., 2016). However, instead of directly incorporating time in the learned embeddings, the method proposed in (Jiang et al., 2016) first learns temporal order among relations (e.g., wasBorIn → wonPrize → diedIn). These relation orders are then incorporated as constraints during the KG embedding stage. Thus, the embedding learned by (Jiang et al., 2016) is not explicitly temporally aware.
我們注意到，知識圖譜中的信念並非普遍為真，因為它們往往只在特定的時間段內有效。例如，（比爾·克林頓，美國總統）僅在 1993 年至 2001 年期間為真。帶有此類時間有效性標記的知識圖譜信念被稱為時間範圍限定的。這些時間範圍在幾個大型知識圖譜中越來越常見，例如 YAGO (Suchanek et al., 2007)、Wikidata (Erxleben et al., 2014)。主流的知識圖譜嵌入方法在學習知識圖譜中節點和關係的嵌入時，忽略了此類時間範圍的可用性或重要性。這些方法將知識圖譜視為靜態圖，並假設其中包含的信念是普遍為真的。這顯然是不夠的，並且可以想見，在表示學習期間納入時間範圍很可能會產生更好的知識圖譜嵌入。儘管其重要性，時間感知的知識圖譜嵌入是一個相對未被探索的領域。最近，(Jiang et al., 2016) 提出了一種利用時間範圍的知識圖譜嵌入方法。然而，(Jiang et al., 2016) 提出的方法並非直接將時間納入學習的嵌入中，而是首先學習關係之間的時間順序（例如，出生於 → 獲獎 → 逝世於）。然後將這些關係順序作為約束納入知識圖譜嵌入階段。因此，(Jiang et al., 2016) 學到的嵌入並非明確地具有時間感知能力。
In order to overcome this challenge, in this paper, we propose Hyperplane-based Temporally aware KG Embedding (HyTE), a novel KG embedding technique which directly incorporates temporal information in the learned embeddings. HyTE fragments a temporally-scoped input KG into multiple static subgraphs with each subgraph corresponding to a timestamp. HyTE then projects the entities and the relations of each subgraph onto timestamp specific hyperplanes. We learn the hyperplane (normal) vectors and the representation of the KG elements distributed over time jointly. Our contributions are as follows.
為克服此挑戰，本文提出基於超平面的時間感知知識圖譜嵌入 (HyTE)，一種新穎的知識圖譜嵌入技術，可直接將時間資訊納入學習的嵌入中。HyTE 將一個具時間範疇的輸入知識圖譜，切割成多個靜態子圖，每個子圖對應一個時間戳。HyTE 接著將每個子圖的實體與關係，投射到特定於該時間戳的超平面上。我們共同學習超平面（法線）向量以及隨時間分佈的知識圖譜元素的表示。我們的貢獻如下：
*   We draw attention to the important but relatively unexplored problem of temporally aware Knowledge Graph (KG) embedding. In particular, we propose HyTE, a temporally aware method for learning Knowledge Graph (KG) embedding.
*   In contrast to previous time-sensitive KG embedding methods, HyTE encodes temporal information directly in the learned embeddings. This enables us to predict temporal scopes for previously unscoped KG beliefs.
*   Through extensive experiments on multiple real-world datasets, we demonstrate HyTE's effectiveness.
*   我們關注到重要但相對未被探索的時間感知知識圖譜 (KG) 嵌入問題。特別是，我們提出了 HyTE，一種用於學習知識圖譜 (KG) 嵌入的時間感知方法。
*   與以往的時間敏感 KG 嵌入方法相比，HyTE 將時間資訊直接編碼在學習到的嵌入中。這使我們能夠預測先前無時間範圍的 KG 信念的時間範圍。
*   透過在多個真實世界數據集上的廣泛實驗，我們證明了 HyTE 的有效性。
We have made HyTE's source code and datasets used in the paper available at https://github.com/malllabiisc/HyTE
我們已將 HyTE 的原始碼及論文中使用的資料集公開於 https://github.com/malllabiisc/HyTE

## 2 Related Work
Temporal fact and event extraction: Time, apart from being an information, also introduces a separate dimension to knowledge. Thus temporal scoping of relational facts is an imperative part of automatic knowledge graph construction and completion. T-YAGO (Wang et al., 2010) extracts temporal facts from semi-structured data like Wikipedia Infoboxes, and categories using only regular expressions. On the other hand, systems like PRAVDA harvests temporal information from free text sources using label propagation. CoTS (Talukdar et al., 2012b) uses integer linear program based approach to model temporal constraints and proposes joint inference framework with few seed examples.
時間事實與事件抽取：時間，除了作為一種資訊外，也為知識引入了一個獨立的維度。因此，關係事實的時間範疇界定是自動化知識圖譜建構與補全中不可或缺的一環。T-YAGO (Wang et al., 2010) 從維基百科資訊框等半結構化資料中，僅使用正規表示式來抽取時間性事實與類別。另一方面，像 PRAVDA 這樣的系統則利用標籤傳播，從自由文本來源中獲取時間資訊。CoTS (Talukdar et al., 2012b) 採用基於整數線性規劃的方法來模型化時間限制，並提出了一個使用少量種子範例的聯合推論框架。
A method for discovering temporal ordering among factual relations was proposed in (Talukdar et al., 2012a). The task of extracting temporally rich events and time expressions and ordering between them is introduced in TempEval challenge (UzZaman et al., 2013; Verhagen et al., 2010). Various approaches (McDowell et al., 2017; Mirza and Tonelli, 2016) made for solving the task proved to be effective in other temporal reasoning tasks. Although we try to attend to a similar problem, the method proposed in this paper is more related to relational embedding learning paradigm than scoping temporal facts from the web.
(Talukdar et al., 2012a) 提出了一種發現事實關係間時間順序的方法。TempEval 挑戰 (UzZaman et al., 2013; Verhagen et al., 2010) 介紹了提取富含時間性事件和時間表達式並對其進行排序的任務。為解決該任務而提出的各種方法 (McDowell et al., 2017; Mirza and Tonelli, 2016) 已被證明在其他時間推理任務中是有效的。儘管我們試圖解決一個類似的問題，但本文提出的方法更多地與關係嵌入學習範式相關，而不是從網絡中界定時間事實的範圍。
Relational embedding learning methods: An enormous amount of research has been done in this field, especially for KG completion or link prediction task (Bordes et al., 2013). (Nickel et al., 2016a) provides a detailed review of the recent KG embedding learning methods. These can be broadly categorized into two different paradigms. TransE(Bordes et al., 2013), TransH(Wang et al., 2014), TransR (Lin et al., 2015), TransD (Ji et al., 2015) are the translational distance-based models. Here the main theme is to minimize the distance between two entity vectors where one of them is translated by a relation vector. The realm of matrix factorization based methods includes bilinear model RESCAL (Nickel et al., 2011), DistMult (Yang et al., 2014), HoIE (Nickel et al., 2016b). Some of the other notable models are Neural Tensor Networks(NTN) (Socher et al., 2013). We also provide some background on the traditional methods in section 3. However, the temporal dimension remains silent in all of these inference methods.
關係嵌入學習方法：在這個領域已經進行了大量的研究，特別是針對知識圖譜補全或鏈接預測任務 (Bordes et al., 2013)。(Nickel et al., 2016a) 對近期的知識圖譜嵌入學習方法進行了詳細的回顧。這些方法大致可以分為兩種不同的範式。TransE (Bordes et al., 2013)、TransH (Wang et al., 2014)、TransR (Lin et al., 2015)、TransD (Ji et al., 2015) 是基於平移距離的模型。這裡的主題是最小化兩個實體向量之間的距離，其中一個向量由一個關係向量平移。基於矩陣分解的方法領域包括雙線性模型 RESCAL (Nickel et al., 2011)、DistMult (Yang et al., 2014)、HoIE (Nickel et al., 2016b)。其他一些著名的模型是神經張量網絡 (NTN) (Socher et al., 2013)。我們也在第 3 節中提供了一些關於傳統方法的背景知識。然而，在所有這些推斷方法中，時間維度都保持沉默。
Link prediction through embeddings of the graph nodes and edges, are not only useful for inference over KG but also important for predicting incomplete pieces of the KG itself. Learning temporally steered embeddings is an important but proportionately less explored problem. Only some handful of methods have been proposed for this purpose. t-TransE (Jiang et al., 2016) learns time aware embedding by learning relation ordering jointly with TransE. They try to inflict temporal order on time-sensitive relations e.g. was BornIn → wonPrize → diedIn. t-TransE does not use the time information directly, whereas we incorporate time directly in our learning algorithm. Another approach Know-Evolve (Trivedi et al., 2017) models the non-linear temporal evolution of KG elements using bilinear embedding learning method. They deploy recurrent neural network to capture non-linear dynamical characteristics of the embeddings. However, they restrict their domain to event-based interaction type of datasets which are fairly dense in nature. Leblay and Chekol (2018) propose a method for temporal embedding learning using side information from the atemporal part of the graph. However, we use purely temporal KG to learn the temporally aware embedding.
透過圖譜節點和邊的嵌入進行連結預測，不僅有助於在知識圖譜上進行推論，對於預測知識圖譜本身不完整的片段也相當重要。學習具備時間導向的嵌入是個重要但相對較少被探討的問題。為此，僅有少數幾種方法被提出。t-TransE (Jiang et al., 2016) 透過與 TransE 共同學習關係排序，來學習具備時間感知能力的嵌入。他們試圖對具時間敏感性的關係施加時間順序，例如 wasBornIn → wonPrize → diedIn。t-TransE 並未直接使用時間資訊，而我們的學習演算法則是直接整合時間。另一種方法 Know-Evolve (Trivedi et al., 2017) 則使用雙線性嵌入學習方法，來模擬知識圖譜元素的非線性時間演化。他們部署了遞歸神經網路來捕捉嵌入的非線性動態特性。然而，他們將其領域限制在互動本質上相當密集的事件型資料集。Leblay 和 Chekol (2018) 提出一種使用圖譜非時間部分的附加資訊來學習時間嵌入的方法。然而，我們純粹使用時間性知識圖譜來學習具備時間感知能力的嵌入。

## 3 Background: KG Embedding
In this section, we provide an overview of the existing methods for knowledge graph representation learning (Bordes et al., 2013), (Wang et al., 2014). Consider a KG G with a set of entities 8. The set of directed edges, D+ consists of triples (h, r, t), where the edge direction is from h to t and the edge label (also popularly known as relation) is r.
在本節中，我們將概述現有的知識圖譜表示學習方法（Bordes et al., 2013）、（Wang et al., 2014）。考慮一個知識圖譜 G，其中包含一組實體 8。有向邊集合 D+ 由三元組（h, r, t）組成，其中邊的方向是從 h 到 t，邊的標籤（通常也稱為關係）是 r。

### 3.1 TransE and TransH
TransE (Bordes et al., 2013) is a simple and efficient translational distance model. It interprets the relation as a translation vector between head and tail entity vectors. Given two entity vectors eh, et ∈ R, it tries to map the relation as a translation vector er ∈ Rn, i.e., en + er ≈ et for observed triple (h, r, t). So the distance based scoring function used for plausible triples is hereby, f(h,r,t) = ||en + er – et||11/12, where, || . ||11/12 is the 11 or 12-norm of the difference vector. f(h,r,t) will be minimized for observed or correct triples. In order to differentiate between correct and incorrect triples, their TransE score difference is minimized using margin based pairwise ranking loss. More formally, we optimize ΣΣ max(0, f(x) − f(y) + y), xED+ yED- with respect to the entity and relation vectors. y is a margin separating correct and incorrect triples. D+ is the set of all positive triples, i.e., observed triples in KG. The negative samples are drawn randomly from the set - D¯ = {(h',r,t)|h' ∈ &, (h',r,t) ¢ D+} ∪{(h,r,t')\t' ∈ &, (h,r,t') ¢ D+}. TransE fails to model the many-to-one, one-to-many, many-to-many type of relations as it does not learn a distributed representation of entities when it is involved with many relations.
TransE (Bordes et al., 2013) 是一個簡單而高效的平移距離模型。它將關係詮釋為頭實體向量和尾實體向量之間的平移向量。給定兩個實體向量 eh, et ∈ R，它試圖將關係映射為一個平移向量 er ∈ Rn，即對於觀察到的三元組 (h, r, t)，en + er ≈ et。因此，用於合理三元組的基於距離的評分函數為 f(h,r,t) = ||en + er – et||11/12，其中 || . ||11/12 是差向量的 11 或 12 範數。對於觀察到的或正確的三元組，f(h,r,t) 將被最小化。為了區分正確和不正確的三元組，它們的 TransE 分數差異使用基於邊界的成對排序損失進行最小化。更正式地，我們對實體和關係向量進行優化 ΣΣ max(0, f(x) − f(y) + y), xED+ yED-。y 是一個分隔正確和不正確三元組的邊界。D+ 是所有正三元組的集合，即在知識圖譜中觀察到的三元組。負樣本從集合 - D¯ = {(h',r,t)|h' ∈ &, (h',r,t) ¢ D+} ∪{(h,r,t')\t' ∈ &, (h,r,t') ¢ D+} 中隨機抽取。TransE 無法模擬多對一、一對多、多對多的關係類型，因為當實體涉及多個關係時，它無法學習實體的分布式表示。

## 4 Proposed Method: HyTE
In this section, we present a detailed description of HyTE (Figure 1) which not only exploits the relational properties among entities but also uses the temporal meta-data associated with them.
在本節中，我們將詳細介紹 HyTE（圖 1），它不僅利用了實體間的關係屬性，也使用了與之相關的時間元數據。

### 4.1 Temporal Knowledge Graph
Usually knowledge graphs are treated as a static graph consisting of triples in form of (h,r,t). Adding a separate time dimension to the triple makes the KG dynamic. Consider the quadruple (h,r,t, [Ts, Te]), where Ts and Te denote the start and end time during which the triple (h, r, t) is valid. Unlike (Jiang et al., 2016), we incorporate this time meta-fact directly into our learning algorithm to learn temporal embeddings of the KG elements. Given the timestamps, the graph can be dismantled into several static graphs consisting of triples that are valid in the respective time steps, e.g., knowledge graph G can be expressed as G = G₁₁ U G₁₂ U • U GTT, where Ti, i ∈ 1,2,…, T are the discrete time points. We constructed this temporal component-graphs (G7) from the quadruples by considering (h,r,t) to be a positive triple at each time point between Ts and Te. Now, given a quadruple (h, r,t, [Ts, Te]), we consider it to be a positive triple for each time point between T, and Te. So, we include (h,r,t) in each G₁, where Ts ≤ t ≤ Te. The set of positive triple corresponding to time 7 is denoted as D‡.
通常，知識圖譜被視為由 (h,r,t) 形式的三元組組成的靜態圖。為三元組添加一個獨立的時間維度，使得知識圖譜具有動態性。考慮四元組 (h,r,t, [Ts, Te])，其中 Ts 和 Te 表示三元組 (h, r, t) 有效的開始和結束時間。與 (Jiang et al., 2016) 不同，我們將此時間元事實直接納入我們的學習算法中，以學習知識圖譜元素的時態嵌入。給定時間戳，該圖可以被分解為幾個由在各自時間步長內有效的三元組組成的靜態圖，例如，知識圖譜 G 可以表示為 G = G₁₁ U G₁₂ U • U GTT，其中 Ti, i ∈ 1,2,…, T 是離散的時間點。我們通過將 (h,r,t) 視為在 Ts 和 Te 之間的每個時間點都是一個正三元組，從四元組中構建了這個時間成分圖 (G7)。現在，給定一個四元組 (h, r,t, [Ts, Te])，我們認為它在 T, 和 Te 之間的每個時間點都是一個正三元組。因此，我們將 (h,r,t) 包含在每個 G₁ 中，其中 Ts ≤ t ≤ Te。對應於時間 7 的正三元組集合表示為 D‡。

### 4.2 Projected-Time Translation
TransE considers entity and relation vectors in the same semantic space for a static graph. We observe that time is the main source of different many-to-one, one-to-many or many-to-many relations, e.g., (h, r) pair can be associated with different tail entity t at different points of time. Thus traditional methods fail to disambiguate them directly. In our time guided model, we want the entity to have a distributed representation associated with different time points. We represent time as a hyperplane i.e., for T number of time steps in the KG, we will have T different hyperplanes represented by normal vectors Wt1, Wt2,……, Wtr. Thus, we try to segregate the space into different time zones with the help of the hyperplanes. Now, triples valid at time T (i.e., the sub graph G₁) are projected onto time specific hyperplane w₁, where their translational distance (TransE Section 3 our case) is minimized. ### 5.1 Datasets
Knowledge Graphs such as Wikidata (Erxleben et al., 2014) and YAGO (Suchanek et al., 2007) have time annotations on a subset of the facts. We extracted the temporally rich subgraph from them for testing our algorithm as well as the baselines. YAGO11k: In the YAGO3 knowledge graph (Mahdisoltani et al., 2013), some temporally associated facts have meta-facts as (#factID, occurSince, ts), (#factID, occurUntil, te). The total number of time annotated facts containing both occursSince and occursUntil are 722,494. Out of them, we selected top 10 most frequent temporally rich relations. In order to handle sparsity, we recursively remove edges containing entity with only a single mention in the subgraph. This ensures a healthy connectivity within the graph. Finally, we obtain a purely temporal graph of 20.5k triples and 10,623 entities by following this procedure. Wikidata12k: We extracted this temporal Knowledge Graph from a preprocessed dataset of Wikidata proposed by (Leblay and Chekol, 2018)¹. We followed a similar procedure as described in YAGO11k. Here also, we distill out the subgraph with time mentions for both start and end. We ensure that no entity has only a single edge connected to it. We select top 24 frequent temporally rich relations for this case, which resulted in 40k triples with 12.5k entities. The dataset is almost double in size with respect to YAGO11k.

### 5.4 Relation Prediction
The aim of this task is to predict the relation between two entities, i.e, for a given time-stamped triple with missing relation (h,?,t,r), we predict the relation r. ### 5.5 Temporal Scope Prediction
Given the scarcity of time annotations of the KG facts, predicting time for atemporal part of the KG is an important problem. Unlike the previous baseline methods, our model can predict the time scope for a given triple. In order to perform better in this task, we want the hyperplanes to be well separated even after maintaining consistency with the positive triples. To incorporate this nature during training, we use the time-dependent negative sampling technique (TDNS Equation 2). Rest of the training procedure remains the same as the link prediction task. The model is trained on the same train split used for link prediction. In this task, we predict the time interval or the time instance 7 for a given test triple (h, r, t,?). We project the relation and the entities of the triple on all the time hyperplanes and check the plausibility of that test triple on each of them. For evaluation, we order the time frames in increasing order of their plausibility score for that particular triple. Now, we select the rank of the time(7) associated with the test triple. If the associated time is an interval, we consider the lowest rank among the times in between the interval.
鑑於知識圖譜事實的時間標註稀少，預測知識圖譜非時間部分的時態範圍是一個重要的問題。與先前的基線方法不同，我們的模型可以預測給定三元組的時態範圍。為了在此任務中表現得更好，我們希望即使在與正三元組保持一致性之後，超平面也能被良好地分離。為了在訓練中納入此特性，我們使用時間相關的負採樣技術（TDNS 方程式 2）。其餘的訓練過程與連結預測任務相同。模型在用於連結預測的相同訓練分割上進行訓練。在此任務中，我們預測給定測試三元組 (h, r, t, ?) 的時間間隔或時間實例 7。我們將三元組的關係和實體投影到所有時間超平面上，並檢查該測試三元組在每個超平面上的合理性。為了評估，我們根據該特定三元組的合理性得分的遞增順序對時間框架進行排序。現在，我們選擇與測試三元組相關聯的時間（7）的排名。如果相關聯的時間是一個區間，我們考慮該區間內時間的最低排名。

## 6 Results
Implementation details: For all the methods, we have kept batch size b = 50k on both the datasets. The dimensions of the embeddings (d) are varied in the range {64, 128, 256}. The margins(n) for all the methods are chosen from the set {1, 2, 5, 10}. Learning rate used for SGD, Ir ∈ {0.01, 0.001, 0.0001}. The best configuration is chosen by corresponding lowest MR on the validation set. For both YAGO11k and Wikidata12k, we obtained d = 128, η = 10, lr = 0.0001 using l₁-norm in the scoring function. Both YAGO11k and Wikidata12k contain time annotations to the granularity of days. For the temporal scoping task, we only deal with year level granularity by dropping the month and date information. Timestamps are then treated as 61 and 78 different intervals for YAGO and Wikidata respectively. The main motive behind having time classes is to distribute the time annotations in the KG uniformly. For example, less frequent year mentions are clubbed into same time class but years with high frequency forms individual classes. We take care of the unbalance that may occur in terms of number of triples in a particular interval by applying a minimum threshold of 300 triples per interval during construction. To illustrate, in Wikidata there are classes like 1596-1777, 1791-1815 with a large span as the events occurring on those points of time are quite less in KG. The years like 2013, 2014 being highly frequent are self-contained.

## 7 Conclusion
We propose HyTE, a hyperplane-based method for learning temporally aware knowledge graph embeddings. Our method exploits temporally scoped facts of KG to perform link prediction as well as prediction of time scopes for unannotated temporal facts. Through extensive experiments on real-world datasets, we demonstrate effectiveness of HyTE over both traditional and time aware embedding methods. In future, we would like to incorporate type consistency information to further improve our model and also integrate HyTE with open-world knowledge graph completion (Shi and Weninger, 2018). We are hopeful that our proposed temporal representation learning algorithm will motivate further research on temporal KG embedding learning.
我們提出了 HyTE，一種基於超平面的方法，用於學習具有時間感知能力的知識圖譜嵌入。我們的方法利用知識圖譜中具有時間範圍的事實來執行連結預測，以及預測未標註時間事實的時間範圍。透過在真實世界資料集上的廣泛實驗，我們證明了 HyTE 相對於傳統和時間感知嵌入方法的有效性。未來，我們希望納入類型一致性資訊以進一步改善我們的模型，並將 HyTE 與開放世界知識圖譜補全（Shi 和 Weninger，2018）相整合。我們希望我們提出的時間表示學習演算法將能激發更多關於時間知識圖譜嵌入學習的研究。
